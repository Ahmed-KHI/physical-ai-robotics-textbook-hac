Think of ROS 2 as the nervous system of a robot - it enables different parts (sensors, actuators, controllers) to communicate and coordinate seamlessly.

## ğŸ§  What is ROS 2?

ROS 2 is not an operating system despite its name. It's a **middleware framework** that provides:

- **Communication infrastructure** between robot components
- **Tools and libraries** for common robotics tasks
- **Standardized interfaces** for sensors and actuators
- **Development and debugging tools** for robot applications
